About Us

Safe World Telecom Ltd is a prominent telecommunications provider in Kenya, dedicated to delivering affordable and reliable communication services. As an authorized Safaricom Dealer and M-Pesa Agent, we empower individuals and businesses in underserved regions by ensuring connectivity and access to essential mobile and financial solutions. Our focus on innovation, customer-centric approach, and market expansion have established us as a trusted leader in the telecom industry.

Our Mission

Our mission is to empower communities through accessible telecommunications services that foster connectivity and economic growth. We aim to expand our network to reach underserved regions, providing modern communication tools and digital financial solutions.

Our Vision

To become a leading provider of telecommunications and digital financial services across East Africa, driving impactful change by connecting people, businesses, and communities through innovative and accessible solutions.
